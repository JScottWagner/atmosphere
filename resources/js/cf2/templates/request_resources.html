Depending on the cloud provider, allocations may be expressed as CPUs, RAM, Atmosphere Units (AUs), or a combination of these. One Atmosphere Unit is roughly equivalent to a CPU-hour, or using one CPU core for one hour. A 16 core instance running for one hour will use 16 AUs. Instances are available on a first-come-first-served basis. Depending on Atmosphere's capacity, you may be unable to launch an instance even though you have a sufficient allocation.
